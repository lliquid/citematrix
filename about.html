Introduction

CiteMatrix is an overview of the citation links among VIS conference papers. The citation links are aggregated by the years and the venues of the papers making a citation and the papers being cited. The sizes of the circle in the matrix are logrithmically proportional to the total number of citation links from the papers published in a particular year in one of the three conferences (InfoVis, VAST, SciVIs) to the papers published in an ealier year in some conference.

Findings

There is a stronger tendency of SciVis papers to cite those published in recent years. VAST and InfoVis papers cite each other more frequently than SciVis.

More

The papers being cited, and the number of citations they received are displayed upon hovering or clicking on the circles. You can use the visualization to find papers with far reaching influence over years and across different areas of visualization research. You can also search by authors and the corresponding cells within the matrix will be highlighted.
